Dimitri Bilov, born in Russia in 1967, graduated from the Art Academy for Theatre and Film in Kazakhstan as an actor, director and set designer. Throughout his life he gathered experiences in different areas. He worked as a soldier, sailor, construction worker and cook.
His first exhibition took place in Almaty in 1989. Since then he took part in several exhibitions in Kazakhstan, Russia, Japan, USA, Germany and France. He lives and works as an actor, director, acting teacher and painter in Cologne, Germany.
